The Restaurant

Cafe Ranch is located in the heart of North Bangalore's Yelahanka Main Road spread over 1 Acre. We're a contemporary themed outdoor cafe which has a dance floor, guest rooms and lawn to host over 100 people, we're know to host anything that calls for a gathering and celebration like birthday's anniversaries, festivals and events.

We conceptualised this cafe keeping the nature and greenery at the centre. We're surrounded by tree's and shrubs all around and we only use eco friendly and durable items to preserve our environment. Cafe Ranch customises themmed celebrations and has bon fire and barbeque on request.
